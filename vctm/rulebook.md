# Demo Credentials Rulebook

## Scope

This rulebook governs the demo credentials published by the SIROS Foundation
for testing and development purposes.

## Credential Types

| Credential | LoS | Binding |
|---|---|---|
| Demo 1 | Low | cnf |
| Diploma | Substantial | cnf |
| EHIC | High | cnf |
| PID (ARF 1.5) | High | cnf |

## Issuance Policy

These credentials are issued for **demonstration purposes only** and carry no
legal or operational validity. They are intended to support interoperability
testing across wallet implementations.

## Trust Framework

Demo credentials are not part of any production trust framework. They follow
the TS11 attestation catalogue format for structural compliance testing.
